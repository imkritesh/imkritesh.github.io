Dirty Coffee

A bold contrast of hot espresso over cold milk.

  • Mix 200 ml of cold milk with 30 ml of condensed milk.
  • Keep the mixture in freezer for 15 minutes.
  • Use dark roast coffee to pull psuedo espresso shot using Aeropress (18 gm coffee brewed with 60 gm water).
  • Gently layer hot espresso on top of cold milk. Enjoy the contrast.

Japanese Iced V60 Coffee

My go to drink during summers

  • Use a coffee-to-water ratio of 1:16. Light roast go well with this method.
  • Grind the beans slightly finer than your usual V60 grind, as we’ll be using less water (Timemore C2: 18–22 clicks).
  • Place 40% of the total water as ice in the carafe.
  • Pour the remaining 60% as hot water over the coffee to brew directly onto the ice.
